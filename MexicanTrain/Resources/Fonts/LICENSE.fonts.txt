  Licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0.
  Source: https://github.com/google/fonts/tree/main/apache/specialelite
  Full license: ./SPECIALELITE_LICENSE.txt

PostScript names ("Rye-Regular", "SpecialElite-Regular") match the entries
in Theme.swift so `UIFont(name:size:)` resolves them directly. Fonts are
registered at launch via `Fonts.registerBundledFonts()`; we do not list
them in the Info.plist's UIAppFonts because the runtime registration path
also works for Dynamic Type.
